Postpartum Recovery Program

A 6-month structured program designed to help you heal your body, restore energy, and achieve safe, sustainable weight balance after childbirth.

Program Overview

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Goal: Support recovery, energy restoration, and healthy postnatal weight balance
  • Designed to support both physical and emotional healing after childbirth
  • Gradual and safe approach respecting your body’s recovery timeline

Phase 1: Early Recovery (0–3 Months)

  • Recovery-focused postnatal nutrition
  • Gentle healing yoga practices
  • Support for fatigue, sleep deprivation, and mood changes
  • Focus on nourishment and tissue healing

Phase 2: Strength & Balance (3–6 Months)

  • Weight management–focused nutrition
  • Strength-building and toning yoga
  • Gradual increase in physical activity
  • Support for sustainable fat loss or body balance

Nutrition & Lifestyle Support

  • Personalised postnatal nutrition plans
  • Meal plan flexibility based on daily routine
  • Lactation-supportive and mood-balancing nutrition
  • Digestive and hormonal balance support

Postnatal Yoga & Movement

  • Safe postnatal yoga sessions (starting after 6 weeks postpartum)
  • Gradual core strengthening and pelvic floor support
  • Improved flexibility and posture correction
  • Energy-restoring and calming practices

Ongoing Guidance & Support

  • One-on-one weekend progress review call
  • Continuous WhatsApp support on weekdays
  • Plan updates based on recovery progress
  • Guidance for physical and emotional changes

Results You Can Expect

  • Stronger core and pelvic floor
  • Improved sleep quality and energy levels
  • Better emotional balance and confidence
  • Healthy and sustainable postnatal weight management
