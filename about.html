Hi, I’m Shaily Jain

Nutritionist | Certified Pregnancy Yoga Expert | Mother

I am a Nutritionist and Certified Pregnancy Yoga Expert — but before any title, I am a mother. My journey into pregnancy wellness did not begin in a classroom. It began in real life.

Before becoming a nutritionist and pregnancy yoga expert, I went through a high-risk pregnancy myself. I experienced gestational diabetes, low amniotic fluid levels, a premature delivery, and ultimately a C-section.

During this time, I was also managing an MBA degree and a full-time job, trying to navigate pregnancy with limited practical guidance. Like many Indian women, I was surrounded by advice — but very little clear, evidence-based, and supportive knowledge.

That experience changed the way I looked at pregnancy. Instead of accepting complications as “normal” or “inevitable,” I chose to educate myself deeply in nutrition and pregnancy-safe yoga to truly support a mother’s body.

Over the last 3+ years, I have seen one powerful truth again and again:

With the right knowledge, timely guidance, and consistent support, many pregnancy complications can be prevented, managed, or significantly reduced.

My Philosophy & Approach

Real & Practical Nutrition

I believe nutrition should fit into your real life — not the other way around. No extreme diets, no food fear, and no unrealistic routines.

Woman-Centric Care

Every stage of a woman’s life is different. My guidance is always personalised, empathetic, and focused on hormonal balance and overall wellbeing.

Mind–Body Balance

Nutrition, gentle yoga, breathwork, and lifestyle habits work together — not separately — for long-term health.
