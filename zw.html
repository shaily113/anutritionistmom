1-Month Holistic Weight Management Program

Modern professional women often spend long hours sitting, managing work stress, irregular meals, poor sleep, and very little time for self-care. Over time, this lifestyle can lead to weight gain, difficulty losing fat, low energy, poor digestion, or even difficulty gaining healthy weight.

As a Nutritionist and Yoga Practitioner, this program is designed specifically for sedentary lifestyle professional women who want structured, realistic, and sustainable results — without crash dieting or exhausting workout routines.

This is a goal-based, personalised program focused on: Weight Loss and Healthy Weight Gain.

What You Will Get (Common for Both Programs)

  • Detailed initial assessment (diet, work routine, lifestyle, sleep, stress, medical history)
  • Weekly updated customised diet plans
  • Daily 1-hour guided yoga sessions
  • WhatsApp support (weekdays) for meal and routine guidance
  • Sunday progress review call
  • Weight and inch change tracking
  • Energy levels and digestion review
  • Regular plan adjustments as per progress
  • Office-friendly food swaps and lifestyle corrections
  • Expert guidance from a Nutritionist and Yoga Practitioner
