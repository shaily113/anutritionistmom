Prenatal Care Program

A 9-month, trimester-wise holistic program designed to support a healthy, calm, and well-nourished pregnancy for both mother and baby.

Program Overview

  • Duration: 9 Months (Trimester-wise support)
  • Goal: Support a healthy, calm, and balanced pregnancy
  • Designed to adapt with your body’s changing needs
  • Focus on nutrition, movement, comfort, and emotional wellbeing

Nutrition & Lifestyle Support

  • Trimester-specific nutrition plans based on health needs and cravings
  • Meal plan adjustments based on daily routine and convenience
  • Nutritional support for nausea, constipation, fatigue, and digestion
  • Lifestyle guidance for energy management and comfort

Prenatal Yoga & Movement

  • Safe, guided prenatal yoga on weekdays
  • Yoga customised for each trimester
  • Improved flexibility, stamina, and circulation
  • Labour-preparation yoga in the final trimester

Ongoing Guidance & Support

  • One-on-one weekend progress review call
  • Continuous WhatsApp support on weekdays
  • Plan updates as pregnancy progresses
  • Guidance for physical and emotional changes

Results You Can Expect

  • Healthy and balanced weight gain
  • Improved stamina and flexibility
  • Reduced pregnancy-related discomforts
  • Emotional calm and confidence
  • A more peaceful pregnancy journey
